About

Your Cybersecurity Mentoring

Who Are We?

We are seasoned information security professionals committed to mentoring professionals in information technology and security. Our individualized training is informed by more than 25 years of experience in cybersecurity from the federal government, academia, and the private sector.

What We Do?

Cyfidant develops on-the-job training so that mentees are trained in the environment in which they will safeguard their company's assets. Instead of simply watching videos, reading books, or attending bootcamps and then attempting to figure out how to use what they've learned in their organization, we will provide guidance on how to incorporate the new skills into the construction of a comprehensive cybersecurity program.

Why We Do It?

Existing training is frequently generic, leaving students to figure out on their own how to execute what they have learned. The majority of training focuses on skill development, but not on program maintenance. Cybersecurity principles are taught to mentees at their workplace.

Who Do We Serve

The ideal Cyfidant mentee(s) is/are a person assuming cybersecurity responsibilities, a person transitioning into cybersecurity with limited to no expertise in doing cybersecurity analyst activities, or a company establishing a new cybersecurity team or security operations center.

Services

what we offer

Secure Communication

All online interactions with mentees will take place on a secure communication platform that employs end-to-end encryption. Secure communication will be utilized for all file transfers, chat support, audio and video conversations.

Customized Training

Each mentee will receive a personalized training program tailored to their organizational positions. As required, we will provide a virtual training environment for testing security procedures in a secure environment prior to their implementation within the organization.

Mentoring

As each mentee applies security controls in their environment, Cyfidant mentors will be available to assist them. Prior to the implementation of security controls, we will provide coaching on testing security controls and create an implementation plan. At Cyfidant, we prefer not to have accounts or access to information assets. All mentees will receive hands-on experience integrating security controls into their organization, although with our guidance at every step.

Documentation

We will create your system security plan and security control testing strategy. These materials will shorten the time required for your organization to onboard new workers and inform them on your security program.

Meet Cyfidant's Founder And Your Mentor

Duane Dunston is an Associate Professor of Cybersecurity at Champlain College. He has been in information security since 1997 working in both the education and government sectors. While in the government, he worked as a security analyst and incident responder. He focuses on risk management, practical use of cryptography, security education, security control testing, and threat hunting.

In addition to teaching and consulting, he is a mentor of the Vermont Cyberpatriots Program. Duane took to the TEDx stage and discussed his big idea on how middle and high schoolers can help to fill the cybersecurity employment gap and support small businesses in their local communities. He is currently pursuing his Doctorate of Education at Northeastern University focusing on cognition and learning. Duane is the Senior Adversarial Engineer at Cloud Range where he creates cyber attacks within a live range and trains existing security teams to identify and respond to the attacks.

Duane has spoken for the Western North Carolina, Colorado, and Burlington Vermont Foreign Policy Association Great Decisions series as a guest lecturer on cybersecurity. He has also been featured on WCAX for topics related to cybersecurity and has written articles for VTDigger.

Duane takes a lot of interest in mentoring people transitioning into cybersecurity and newcomers to the industry.
